## Saveliev

- **Chapter 6: A bird's-eye view of basic calculus** (pp. 417–490) — recommended as bonus reading to see how linear algebra threads into calculus (differentiation/integration as linear operators).

- Gilbert Strang, *Introduction to Linear Algebra*, Ch. 7 — the gold-standard SVD treatment.
